Include in a support request

  • your iPhone model and iOS version;
  • Setora version and build number;
  • the action you were performing and what happened;
  • a screenshot if it does not reveal information you prefer not to share.

Do not send passwords, Apple identity tokens, medical records, or other sensitive information. We aim to respond within 7 business days.

Account and data deletion

Guest users can choose “Delete and sign out” in Profile → Account. Apple-linked users can choose “Delete account” in the same section. The app asks for confirmation and then permanently removes the Setora account and associated cloud data. If the in-app action is unavailable, email us with the subject “Setora data deletion”; we may ask for information needed to verify the account.
