2. Information handled by Setora

  • Profile information: display name, gender, height, and weight.
  • Fitness information: training plans, exercise names, sets, repetitions, weights, rest periods, workout dates and duration, cardio distance, speed, incline, and estimated calories.
  • Account information: a Supabase user identifier and, when you choose Sign in with Apple, Apple-provided identity information such as a relay email address and name where Apple makes them available.
  • Technical synchronization information: record identifiers, timestamps, schema version, revision counters, and deletion markers used to back up and restore your data.
  • Support information: the contents of messages and contact details you voluntarily send to us.

3. Local storage and cloud backup

Setora stores your profile and workout data locally on your device. When cloud backup is enabled in the released app, Setora also sends a versioned backup to Supabase. The backup is associated with an anonymous Setora account or with your Apple-linked account and may be used to restore your data after reinstalling the app or signing in on another supported device.

Authentication tokens are stored using device-protected storage, including the iOS Keychain, to preserve and secure your session.

4. Apple Health

If you expressly authorize Apple Health access, Setora writes completed workouts to HealthKit, including workout type, start and end time, and available distance and estimated active-energy values. Setora does not read health data from HealthKit. HealthKit data is not used for advertising, marketing, profiling, or sale, and Setora does not send data obtained from HealthKit to Supabase.

You can manage Health permissions in iOS Settings or the Health app. Removing permission stops future exports but does not remove workouts already saved in HealthKit.

5. Purposes of processing

We use information only to provide app functionality, save and restore your profile and workout progress, authenticate accounts, synchronize data, prevent conflicting backups, respond to support requests, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations. Setora does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not use your data for cross-app tracking.

6. Service providers

We use Supabase for authentication, database hosting, backup, and synchronization, and Apple for Sign in with Apple, HealthKit, App Store distribution, and related platform services. These providers process information under their own terms and privacy commitments. We may also disclose information when required by law or necessary to protect users, Setora, or others.

7. Retention and deletion

Local data remains until you delete it in Setora, delete your profile, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Cloud backups for an Apple-linked account are retained while the account is active so that progress can be restored. Signing out of an Apple-linked account clears local Setora data but preserves its cloud backup.

For a guest profile, choosing “Delete and sign out” removes the Setora cloud profile, backup, and exercise catalog records and clears local app data. Apple-linked users may request permanent deletion of their Setora cloud data by emailing us. We may retain limited records where required for security, dispute resolution, or legal compliance.

8. Your choices and rights

You can edit profile information in the app, decline or revoke Apple Health access, use Setora without linking an Apple account, delete a guest profile in the app, and contact us to request access, correction, export, restriction, or deletion where applicable law grants those rights. We may need to verify your request.

9. Children

Setora is not directed to children under 13, or a higher minimum age where required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below the applicable age.

10. International processing and security

Information may be processed in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including access controls and encrypted transport, but no system can be guaranteed completely secure.

11. Changes

We may update this policy when Setora, our providers, or legal requirements change. We will update the effective date and publish the revised policy on this page.
